A small introduction into me and what I hope to achieve, May 2, 2022

Think. Talk. Teach. These are the founding principles of my blog and the tentative name of my future non-profit I hope to create. Education shouldn't be a priviledge, it should be a right. Yet, it has become a commodity, a luxury good exclusive to those who are able to pay for it, access it, or even just be allowed to expereince it. Through this blog, I hope to expose readers to topics in Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (D.E.I) and voices that have been historically silenced, misrepressented, and opressed.

My name is Jui

I have many thoughts, and I like when people listen to me, so what better idea than to create a blog where any and all people can hear what I think and help me educate myself and others about topics that need to be discussed.
